What's Wispar?

A free, open-source, self-hostable, digital book management solution; Wispar aims to replicate features of restrictive commercial eBook and Audiobook management suites, like those offered by Amazon, Google, Apple etc. The core innovative feature of Wispar is a synced audio-to-text reading experience similar to the one provided by Audible/Kindle Whispersync, without being limited to books purchased from Amazon, devices supported by Amazon, or requiring their monthly subscription The simple and intuitive web interface is accesible in most modern browsers and combines playback progress across audio/ebooks into a single Library item. It also provides some automatic metadata correction or augmentation from online APIs, as well as an interface for users to manually manage book metadata.

Using Wispar

Wispar is a completely free, open source, and self hostable solution to managing your ebook and audiobook library.

Open Source

Wispar is completely free and open source. Features often locked behind a paywall are yours, free of charge.

Privately Hosted

Wispar is run as a self hosted service, meaning you have complete control over who has access to your library.

Highly Configurable

Configure your Wispar instance to your liking, allowing you to run your instance the exact way you prefer.

Feature Rich

Read your ebooks and listen to your audiobooks with Wispar's large number of ebook and audiobook features.
